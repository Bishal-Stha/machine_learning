Environmental Impact Analysis

Understanding Human Impact on Our Planet

Deforestation

Key Fact: 15 billion trees are cut down annually

Main causes include agricultural expansion, logging, and urban development.

Species Extinction

Key Fact: 1 million species at risk of extinction

Current extinction rates are 1000 times higher than natural background rates.

Ocean Pollution

Key Fact: 8 million tons of plastic enter oceans annually

By 2050, there could be more plastic than fish in the oceans by weight.

Air Pollution

Key Fact: 7 million premature deaths annually due to air pollution

91% of the world's population lives in places where air quality exceeds WHO guideline limits.

Solutions & Actions

  • Individual Actions: Reduce plastic use, conserve energy, use public transport
  • Community Level: Support local recycling programs, participate in clean-up drives
  • Government Policy: Implement carbon pricing, protect endangered species
  • Business Actions: Adopt sustainable practices, reduce emissions
